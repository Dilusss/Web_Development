Nuwara Eliiya

Nuwara Eliya, often dubbed "Little England," is a slice of Sri Lankan paradise nestled within emerald hills. With its cool climate, rolling tea estates, and charming colonial architecture, this town transports you to an era of old-world elegance. Wander through the picturesque tea plantations, sip on some of the finest Ceylon tea, or embark on scenic hikes to waterfalls and misty peaks. Nuwara Eliya's timeless charm and natural beauty make it an idyllic escape, where you can relish the serenity of lush landscapes and the warmth of Sri Lankan hospitality.

NuwaraEliya Package
  • Explore Horton Plains National Park: A short drive from Nuwara Eliya, this national park offers the chance to hike to "World's End," a sheer cliff with breathtaking views. You can also visit Baker's Falls and enjoy the park's unique ecosystem.

  • Stroll through Victoria Park: This beautifully landscaped park is a great place for a leisurely walk. It features a wide variety of plants, flowers, and bird species.

  • Visit Seetha Amman Temple:This Hindu temple is steeped in mythology and is said to be connected to the epic Ramayana. It's a serene place for reflection and offers a glimpse into Sri Lanka's diverse religious heritage.

  • Go Horseback Riding: Enjoy a horseback ride through the scenic countryside, with options to explore tea estates, villages, and picturesque trails.

  • Visit a Tea Plantation:Nuwara Eliya is at the heart of Sri Lanka's tea country. We will take you a guided tour of one of the many tea plantations, such as Pedro Tea Estate or Mackwoods, to learn about the tea-making process and enjoy freshly brewed Ceylon tea.
