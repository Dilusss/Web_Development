Galle

Galle, a coastal gem on the southwestern tip of Sri Lanka, is a captivating blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. Enclosed by centuries-old fortifications, the Galle Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that transports you back in time with its colonial-era architecture, cobblestone streets, and charming boutiques. Beyond the fort's walls, you'll find golden beaches where the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean invite you to relax and unwind. Explore the vibrant markets, savor delectable seafood, and immerse yourself in the city's rich heritage. Galle is a captivating destination where the past meets the present, offering a truly enchanting experience on the island's sun-kissed coast.

Galle Package
  • xplore Galle Fort:The Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is the city's main attraction. Wander through the cobblestone streets, admire the Dutch and Portuguese architecture, and explore the historic ramparts that offer stunning views of the ocean.

  • Galle Fort Lighthouse: Visit the iconic Galle Fort Lighthouse, a picturesque spot perfect for photos.

  • Galle Maritime Museum:Learn about the maritime history of Galle and its importance as a trading port through the centuries at the Maritime Museum.

  • Dutch Reformed Church:Explore the Grote Kerk (Great Church) located within the fort. This historic Dutch Reformed church dates back to the 18th century and features impressive colonial-era architecture.

  • Galle National Museum:Discover more about the history and culture of Galle and the southern region of Sri Lanka at the National Museum.

  • Visit the Unawatuna and Jungle beach:Unwind on the beautiful beaches around Galle,which offers opportunities for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

  • Japanese Peace Pagoda:Hike to the Japanese Peace Pagoda located on Rumassala Hill for panoramic views of the coastline and the Indian Ocean.
