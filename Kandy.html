Kandy

andy, the cultural heart of Sri Lanka, beckons with its lush emerald landscapes, sacred relics, and a captivating aura of tradition. Nestled amidst misty hills, this charming city enchants visitors with its UNESCO-listed Temple of the Tooth Relic, a place of deep spiritual significance. Explore the Royal Botanic Gardens, where nature's beauty is meticulously preserved, or embark on a scenic train journey through verdant tea plantations. Kandy's rich heritage, serene lakes, and vibrant markets create a tapestry of experiences that make it a must-visit destination, inviting you to immerse yourself in the soul of Sri Lanka.

Kandy Package
  • Visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa):This UNESCO World Heritage Site houses the sacred tooth relic of Lord Buddha. It's a place of great religious and cultural significance.

  • Explore the Royal Botanic Gardens: Located in nearby Peradeniya, these gardens are renowned for their diverse plant species, including orchids and giant bamboo. Take a leisurely stroll amidst the lush greenery and serene landscapes.

  • Witness a Traditional Kandyan Dance Performance:Experience the vibrant and colorful traditional dances and music of Kandy. These performances often take place in the evenings and offer insight into Sri Lanka's rich culture.

  • Explore Udawatta Kele Sanctuary:This protected forest area offers scenic nature trails where you can spot diverse wildlife, including monkeys and a variety of bird species. It's a peaceful retreat from the city's hustle and bustle.

  • Taste Traditional Kandyan Cuisine:Don't miss the opportunity to savor local dishes like Kandyan chicken curry, string hoppers, and a variety of sweets like Kiri Pani (milk toffee).

Price: 100$ (per person)
