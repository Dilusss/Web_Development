Trincomalee

Explore Trincomalee, where turquoise waters caress pristine white sands, and ancient history whispers through sacred temples and colonial forts. Dive into the vibrant marine world, go whale-watching in the azure depths, or bask in the tranquil hot springs. With a blend of natural wonders and cultural treasures, Trincomalee is an invitation to discover the perfect harmony of paradise and heritage on Sri Lanka's enchanting northeastern coast.

Trincomalee Package
  • Nilaveli Beach: Nilaveli Beach is one of the most famous beaches in Trincomalee.This also include a boat ride to Pigeon Island, a marine national park known for snorkeling and diving.

  • Koneswaram Temple: This ancient Hindu temple,is perched atop a cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean. It's a significant religious and historical site, and the views from the temple are breathtaking

  • Fort Frederick: Located in the heart of Trincomalee, Fort Frederick is a colonial-era fort originally built by the Portuguese and later expanded by the Dutch and the British. You can explore the fort's history and enjoy panoramic views of the city and harbor from its vantage points with our package

  • Whale and Dolphin Watching: Trincomalee is a great place for whale and dolphin watching. Take a boat tour from the harbor, and you may have the chance to spot these magnificent marine creatures in their natural habitat

  • Hot Springs: Kanniya Hot Springs is a set of natural hot springs located a short drive from Trincomalee. It will be a relaxing experience to soak in these therapeutic waters

Price: 100$ (per person)
