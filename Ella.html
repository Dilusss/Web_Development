Ella

Ella, a hidden gem in the Sri Lankan highlands, is a place of untamed beauty and rustic charm. Surrounded by lush tea plantations, this small town offers breathtaking vistas of rolling hills, cascading waterfalls, and dramatic cliffs. The iconic Ella Rock hike beckons adventurers, while the Nine Arch Bridge captures the imagination with its colonial-era engineering marvel. Ella's laid-back atmosphere, cool climate, and friendly locals create a haven for backpackers and nature enthusiasts alike. It's a place where time slows down, allowing you to immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring landscapes and the tranquil rhythm of rural life.

Ella Package
  • Hike to Ella Rock: Trekking to Ella Rock is a must-do activity. The hike offers breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside, tea plantations, and valleys.

  • Visit Nine Arch Bridge:This iconic bridge is a marvel of British colonial engineering. You can watch trains pass over the bridge and take stunning photos of the lush greenery surrounding it.

  • Explore Little Adam's Peak: This shorter and less strenuous hike takes you to a viewpoint with panoramic views of Ella Gap, Ella Rock, and the tea plantations. It's a great option for sunrise or sunset.

  • Ravana Falls:Visit Ravana Falls, one of Sri Lanka's most famous waterfalls. You can take a short walk to the base of the falls for a refreshing swim.

  • Tea Plantation Tours:Ella is surrounded by tea plantations. Take a guided tour to learn about the tea-making process, from plucking the leaves to brewing the perfect cup of Ceylon tea.

  • Lipton's Seat:Named after the famous tea magnate Sir Thomas Lipton, this viewpoint provides stunning vistas of the surrounding tea plantations. It's especially beautiful during sunrise.

Price: 100$ (per person)
